Fighting COVID-19. Better.

VersusVirus enables intuitive, rapid and reproducible quality of care pattern analysis of infectious patients without prior software experience. It facilitates understanding and communication of important trends, performances and patient outcome.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH VersusVirus:

  • Find patients receiving specific treatments, selected by their first prescription and filter or stratify by various groups.
  • Check whether diagnostics have been performed in a given time. 
  • Analyse how long patients stay in hospital. 

Look out for  for additional options in graphs and tables.
